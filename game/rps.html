Rock, Paper, Scissors!

How to play:

Choose player 1 and player 2. Player 1 uses the controls 'q' for rock, 'w' for scissors, and 'e' for paper.
player 2 use 'i' 'o' and 'p' for the same thing.
After you are done selecting, click the ' To Battle!' button to start the battle. The winner will be displayed, and you can start your next match after the animations are finished.
